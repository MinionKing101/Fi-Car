Market Strategy & Launch

Phase 1: Owner Acquisition

Secure 300+ high-quality vehicles by focusing on our core value proposition: guaranteed asset security and superior yield potential.

  • Hook: Zero-deductible options & theft-by-hirer coverage.
  • Channels: Direct outreach to fleet managers, partnerships with dealerships.
  • Action: 30-day "Telematics Installation Blitz" to rapidly build initial supply.

Phase 2: Renter Acquisition

Launch with the campaign theme "Arrive Vetted, Arrive Protected" to attract high-value customers who prioritize safety.

  • Primary Target: Corporate and high-income users via Google Ads & business publications.
  • Secondary Target: Scale volume with locals/tourists via social media & SEO.
  • Messaging: Emphasize trust, transparency, and the seamless digital experience.
